MLCast Datasets

MLCast Datasets is the home for ARCO meteorological data — Analysis-Ready, Cloud-Optimized. ARCO means the data arrives in chunked, cloud-native formats (like Zarr) that you can stream and slice directly into a training loop — no downloading terabytes, no reformatting, no preprocessing overhead. Just open it and train.

Every dataset is curated through our catalog, so you get standardized variables, consistent grids, and rich metadata you can trust. And every dataset is checked by the MLCast validator before it ships, guaranteeing that what you load is complete, well-formed, and reproducible.

We already cover several countries across Europe — and this is just the start. The catalog is open, and we actively encourage you to contribute data from your own region. More coverage means better models for everyone.

MLCast Software

MLCast Software is the toolkit that turns those datasets into working nowcasting models. It ships with a growing catalog of pre-trained models, ready to use out of the box — load one and start forecasting in minutes. And when you want to go further, a unified training pipeline lets you retrain or fine-tune any model on your own data, so going from raw data to a trained model is a matter of configuration, not boilerplate.

We ship benchmarks and baselines out of the box: standardized evaluation suites and reference implementations that let you measure any new architecture against established results on the same data, the same way, every time.

Our goal is an end-to-end workflow — data ingestion, training, validation, and evaluation all stitched together in one open, reproducible stack. Whether you want to reproduce a baseline, push a new model onto the leaderboard, or wire MLCast into your own system, the building blocks are here.
