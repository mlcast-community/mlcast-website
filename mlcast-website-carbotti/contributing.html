[CONTRIBUTE TO MLCAST]

There is more than one way to contribute.

MLCast is a collaborative community advancing weather nowcasting through machine learning by openly sharing datasets, software, experiments, and expertise.

Scientists, researchers, software engineers, and curious builders can all contribute in different ways.
