# Community & News

## Monthly community meetings

Open to anyone, on the first Thursday of the month — updates on development,
new models and datasets, and open discussion. Join the
[mailing list](mailto:mlcastcommunity+subscribe@googlegroups.com) to get the
calendar invite and Teams link.

## Meeting notes

Notes are published after each meeting: topics discussed, decisions,
development progress, and action items, so contributors who couldn't attend
can catch up. See the [Collaborate](collaborate.md) page for how to connect
in the meantime.
