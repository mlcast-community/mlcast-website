What is MLCast?

MLCast Community is a decentralized ecosystem of researchers, engineers, and meteorologists building open-source infrastructure for high-precision weather nowcasting. We leverage modern machine learning techniques to process vast amounts of radar data in real-time, providing critical lead time for severe weather events.

We currently gather radar precipitation across Europe, growing country by country. Drag, scroll, or use the controls to explore where data is live — and where we still need your help.

[MISSION]

Collaborative Research Ecosystem

Our mission is to democratize access to advanced meteorological prediction tools. By fostering an open-source community, we aim to accelerate innovation in weather nowcasting, bridging the gap between academic research and real-world requirements.

  • check_circle Standardized, documented ARCO datasets for Machine Learning weather forecasts.
