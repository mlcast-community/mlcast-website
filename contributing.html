Validate Results

Before any dataset reaches the public catalog, it passes through the dataset validator: an automated check that verifies schema, spatial and temporal coverage, units, and missing-data ratios so every contribution stays consistent and reusable.

The same rigor applies to models. Forecasts are scored against shared baselines with standard nowcasting metrics — MSE, MAE, CRPS, and AFCRPS — across lead times and extreme-rainfall cases, so performance claims are reproducible and comparable.

Reviewing these checks, spotting edge cases, and improving the validator keeps the project on a stronger, more trustworthy foundation. Your critical eye helps the community understand what truly works and what needs improvement.
